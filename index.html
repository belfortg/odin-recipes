BEC

Description

The Bacon, Egg, and Chesse Sandwich, aka the "BEC," is a healthy breakfast option made on a croissant. Its main ingredients include turkey bacon, egg whites, and pepper jack cheese. The combination of these ingredients inside a buttery croissant makes for a delicious meal high in protein and low on calories.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of turkey bacon
  • 1/2 cup of egg whites
  • 1 slice of pepper jack cheese
  • 1 croissant

Steps

  1. Pan-sear 2 slices of turkey bacon for five minutes.
  2. Simultaneously pan-sear 1/2 a cup of egg whites for five minutes.
  3. In the interim, take 1 croissant and slice it in half.
  4. Place turkey bacon, egg whites, and 1 slice of pepper jack cheese in croissant.

MCS

Description

The Mexican Caesar Salad, aka the "MCS," is a healthy lunch option made with grilled chicken and greens. This greens heavy meal is low in calories but very flavorable.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pack of grilled chicken
  • 1/2 pack of lettuce
  • 1 cup of crushed toritilla chips
  • 1/2 cup of jalapenos
  • 1/2 mozzarella cheese
  • Moderate salad dressing

Steps

  1. Rinse greens with cold water.
  2. Add all ingredients into bowl.

SFVG

Description

The Stir Fried Veggies and Chicken meal, aka the "SFVG," is a healthy dinner option made with frozen vegetables, chicken breasts, and quinoa. This meal is high in protein and low on calories.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pack of frozen vegetables
  • 1/2 pack of chicken breasts
  • 1/2 pack of quinoa

Steps

  1. Pan-sear chicken breasts for twenty five to forty minutes.
  2. Boil quinoa for fifteen to thirty minutes.
  3. Pan-sear frozen vegetables for five to ten minutes.
  4. Place cooked ingredients in large bowl and mix.
